Chapter 1 - First war of Independence

1) What do you understand by Subsidary alliance?
Who introduced it? What are the provisions under it?
Name the first king who accepted Subsidary alliance.

Subsidiary alliance was an agreement between the British East India Company and the Indian Princely States by virtue of which these states lost their sovereignty to the British.
It was introduced by Lord Wellesley
The Indian Rulers who agreed to the subsidary alliance-
  • Accepted British as the supreme power
  • surrendered their foreign relationships to the east India Company
  • Accepted a British resident at their headquarters
  • Agreed to maintain british troops at their own costs
  • Virtually lost their Independence
